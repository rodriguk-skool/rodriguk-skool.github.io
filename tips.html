Tips & Fun Facts

  • Red Pikmin are immune to fire.
  • Yellow Pikmin are immune to electricity.
  • Blue Pikmin are immune to water and can’t drown.
  • Rock Pikmin can smash crystal barriers and can’t be crushed.
  • Winged Pikmin can fly and lift items over hazards.
